Jazz music started in the late 19th century in New Orleans, Louisiana with roots in blues and ragtime. Jazz is typically characterized by swing, blue notes, complex chords, polyrhythms, and improvisation. Evan has been playing saxophone for nearly 8 years and found a love for jazz after he took music theory in high school. This webpage has a list of a few of Evan's favorite jazz artists and his top 10 favorite jazz songs.

Favorite Artists

The following is a list of a few of Evan's favorite jazz artists.

  • Gerry Mulligan

  • Maynard Ferguson

  • Stan Getz

  • Michel Camilo

Top 10 Favorite Songs

The following is a list of Evan's favorite jazz songs with links to their respective Youtube videos. Although some of the artists are not listed as favorites above, it does not mean they are not amazing!

  1. Just Kidding - Michel Camilo
  2. Desafinado - Stan Getz
  3. Utter Chaos - Gerry Mulligan
  4. Cruisin' For A Bluessin' - Maynard Ferguson
  5. Bucks County Bounce - Sam "The Man" Taylor
  6. Moanin' - Mingus Big Band
  7. Walkin'shoes - Gerry Mulligan
  8. Don't Let The Sun Go Down On Me - Maynard Ferguson
  9. Just Like You - Michel Camilo
  10. Fine and Mellow - Gerry Mulligan

Words from the author

It was very hard to decide on just 10 songs that I liked the most, there are around 100 jazz songs that I love listening to but it was fun to make a list like this to share on this webpage!
